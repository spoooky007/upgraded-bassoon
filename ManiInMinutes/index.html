Pro Application Tips

Make your press-on nails last over 2 weeks with these expert steps.

🧼

Prep is Key

Always wash hands and wipe nails with alcohol pads before application to remove oils and debris.

✨

Buff Gently

Lightly buff the surface of your natural nail to give the glue a better grip and longer hold.

⏱️

Press and Hold

Apply firm pressure for at least 30 seconds to ensure no air bubbles are trapped underneath.

💧

Avoid Water

Keep nails dry for at least 1-2 hours after application for the strongest bond possible.
